Metrology and Quality

If you can’t measure it, you can’t make it repeatably. Good measurements are consistent and documented.

Back to Learn Open videos on YouTube

Calipers vs micrometers

Calipers are versatile; micrometers give higher resolution. Zero before use, apply consistent pressure, and measure from more than one orientation.

Tolerance and finish

A part can be in‑tolerance but still perform poorly if finish is wrong. Match finish to function.

Inspection planning

List features, instruments, and acceptable ranges. Record nominal, tolerance, and actual measurements, then adjust the process if results drift.
