What you will learn (explained)

  • Finding all energy sources: electrical, pneumatic, hydraulic, mechanical
  • Applying device, hasp, and tag with clear identification
  • Verifying zero energy state by attempting a start before work

Project walkthrough

  1. Choose one machine and list its energy sources
  2. Write a mini procedure with pictures for each step
  3. Have a classmate follow your procedure and give feedback
