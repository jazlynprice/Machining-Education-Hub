Lockout / Tagout Basics

LOTO prevents unexpected startup or energy release. It is a disciplined, step‑by‑step process — no shortcuts.

Back to Learn Open videos on YouTube

Find all energy sources

List electrical, pneumatic, hydraulic, mechanical, thermal, and gravity energy. Many machines have multiple sources — e.g., spindle motor plus pneumatic drawbar. Document each point.

Apply locks and tags

Use the right device for the source and apply a personal lock and tag with your name and date. For group work, use a group hasp or lockbox so every person adds their own lock.

Verify zero energy state

After locking out, attempt a start to confirm nothing can energize. Bleed off stored air or pressure.

Restore to service

Remove tools, confirm guards, notify people nearby, then remove your own lock. Never remove someone else’s lock without a formal process.
