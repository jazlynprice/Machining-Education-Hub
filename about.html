Mission and story

We make technical education approachable and equitable. This hub connects students with clear learning paths, mentors, and real opportunities in machining and manufacturing.

Founder

Hi, I am Jazlyn Price — a student machinist and builder. I created this hub to open doors for other students, especially young women of color, and to carry my brother’s legacy forward by pursuing excellence in everything I build.

Long term, I plan to run a machining and manufacturing company that blends cars, aerospace ideas, and future tech. This hub is step one.
