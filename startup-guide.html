Machine Startup and Shutdown

Checklists reduce mistakes when attention is split between fixturing, offsets, and measurement.

Back to Learn Open videos on YouTube

Startup checklist

Before power‑on: check guards, e‑stop, lubrication, air, and coolant. Verify workholding, program, tools, and WCS. Use single‑block and reduced feed for the first motions.

Shutdown checklist

Return tools, power down, wipe chips, mop spills, reset vises, and sweep the table. Leave the machine ready for the next operator.

Emergency stops & recovery

Practice with your instructor. After an e‑stop, re‑inspect tool, holder, and workholding before resuming. Never rush a restart.
