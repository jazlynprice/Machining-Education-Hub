Module

Personal Protective Equipment

PPE protects your eyes, ears, hands, and feet from common shop hazards. Learn what to wear and when to wear it.

Back to all modules

What you will learn

  • Safety glasses, face shields, and when to use each
  • Hearing protection and noise exposure basics
  • Gloves: when they help and when to avoid around rotating tools
  • Closed-toe / safety toe footwear and shop clothing

Try this project

Create a one-page poster that lists required PPE for your school shop and where to find it.

Checklist

  • Identify PPE required at your station
  • Inspect PPE for damage before use
  • Wear PPE from setup to cleanup
