Housekeeping and Safe Pathways

Clean, organized shops make better parts faster. Treat housekeeping as part of making the part.

Back to Learn Open videos on YouTube

Chip & coolant management

Use chip‑breaking toolpaths when possible. After cutting, sweep chips with a brush and use a chip hook for stringers. Absorb coolant and keep ways clean.

Shadow boards and audits

Shadow boards and labeled storage prevent lost tools and speed setup. End‑of‑class visual audits catch missing items quickly.

Pathways and staging

Mark walkways and keep carts in staging boxes, not aisles. Clear floors prevent trips when carrying stock or moving vises.
