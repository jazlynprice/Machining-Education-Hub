Intro to CNC Milling

Understand coordinate systems, workholding, tooling, and basic toolpaths.

What you will learn

  • Work offsets (G54 etc.) and coordinate systems
  • Workholding: vise, clamps, and soft jaws
  • Tooling basics: end mills, drills, holders
  • Feeds, speeds, and chip load intro

Checklist

  • Choose a tool and enter feeds/speeds
